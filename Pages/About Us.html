Desborde Games is an independent studio that creates videogames with at least one strange idea. We move at a sustainable pace, working step by step in our spare time, keeping the balance between our passion and our life. We bet on creative risk: we explore unconventional mechanics, styles and narratives. And we do it with honesty, sharing both our successes and our mistakes. At Desborde games, we believe that good games are born from curiosity and sincere collaboration.
